About

When I was elementary school, I was always called the art-crazy, tech-savvy child. I often drew on all the notebooks I was given. This led me to winning 3rd Prize for the National Youth Art Competition at the age of six with my drawing titled, Still Life. My artsy beginnings started with the paper then it led to the computer. I designed a website full of childish games and bright colours. As I grew I began breaking apart from the game crazy child and decided to make informative sites relating to the books I read or the subjects I learned in class.

Currently, I work with the people at Mid Michigan Interactive, a website and online marketing company in the Greater Lansing Area where I am the web designer. The art side of myself has not been lost: from pen and paper to computer and mouse, I have created designs for many different companies around the Great Lansing Area.

I currently attend Michigan State University. I am studying Media and Information with a concentration in Interactive and Social Media. I am also working toward a Chinese and Information Technology Minor.
